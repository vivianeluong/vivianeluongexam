POEM 2

Who is there?

Alone in the silent dark

If only there was a spark

A spark which told me I would be okay

Everything just seems so grey

I start to hear footsteps

Is someone at my doorsteps?

Who is there?

I start to blare

Get away from me, I scream

Is this all just some kind of dream?

I can see you

What are you going through?

Why are you here?
