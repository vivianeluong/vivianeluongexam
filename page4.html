COMMUNITY INFORMATIVE

Dear Community,
    Please understand those with a disorder cannot control it. They want to be just like everyone else. They are trying to be like one of us and fit in as much as possible. Yes, some of those who has a disorder may be loud or shout during class, but they cannot control it. It may be hard to not be able to ignore it but just know that they are trying. Thoughts, reality, emotions, behavior, and etc are all different when someone is diagnosed with schizophrenia. Those with this disease usually have it for the rest of their life. Treatment may help the condition but there are no cure for this specific ailment. There should be no judging against them, no name calling, and no bullying, treat them how you wanna be treated. Do not be one of those people who treats them like they are not a person, it is not right to degrade a person. There is so much potential within them, but they cannot tell the difference between what is reality and what is not. They are as well hurt and scared.
