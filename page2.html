AM I THE ONE?

I have had a conversation with the demons

As if it had a reason

Is this all a joke

Or am I having a stroke

I spoke with the god

Why is this so odd

Please tell me this is all an illusion

When can all of this come to a conclusion

All I hear are eccentric commands

It feels like I am sinking in quicksand

Do you see what I see

Because I see a dead sea

Heading out for me
